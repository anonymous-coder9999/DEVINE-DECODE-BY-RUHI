About Ruhi

Guidance rooted in study, not guesswork.

Ruhi Paul has spent years studying astrology, tarot and the Akashic records, and works with clients who want more than a quick answer — they want to understand the pattern behind what they're going through. Every session blends chart reading, card work and intuitive insight, grounded by numerology and, where it's needed, crystal and candle healing to support the energy shift.

Her approach is warm, direct and judgement-free: real answers, practical next steps, and space to ask the questions you've been sitting on.
